Bottletree Hotel is a country pub at the heart of Glamorgan Vale, serving hearty meals, cold beer, and good company since day one. Tucked away from the rush of the city, we've become a local favourite for travellers, families, and regulars alike — the kind of place where you're greeted by name on your second visit and treated like family on your first. Whether you're stopping in after a long ride with the boys, grabbing a feed with the kids after a big weekend, or settling in for a Friday night drink with mates, you'll find a warm welcome, a full plate, and good conversation waiting for you. Our kitchen turns out classic pub favourites done right - generous servings, honest flavours, and nothing fancy, just good food made to be shared.
